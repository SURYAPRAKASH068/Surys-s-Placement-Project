# 🎓 Smart Placement Analytics System (SPAS) 🚀

Welcome to **SPAS** – your ultimate **college placement BFF**!  
This is not just any project… it’s an **AI-powered, explainable, and super-smart placement platform** that makes students, faculty, and placement officers look like superheroes 🦸‍♂️🦸‍♀️.

---

## 🌟 What SPAS Does

- **Students**: Track your marks, attendance, skills, and see if you are **placement-ready** 💼  
- **Faculty**: Monitor student performance, identify weaklings (oops… we mean “opportunity areas” 😅), and generate **reports in a click**  
- **Placement Officers**: Automatically filter **eligible students for any company** & download Excel lists – no more manual chaos! 📊  
- **AI Magic**: Predicts placement probability and explains exactly **why you are ready (or not)** using **Explainable AI** 🧠

---

## 🚀 Key Features (aka Why this project is AWESOME)

- 🎓 **Student Profile & Resume Builder** – Auto PDF download included  
- 📊 **Academic Dashboard** – Grades, attendance, and backlogs at a glance  
- 💼 **Company Eligibility Checker** – Finds out if you can apply, and why  
- 📥 **Excel Export** – Generate company-wise eligible student lists  
- ✉️ **Auto Email Notifications** – Students get alerts automatically  
- 🤖 **AI Placement Predictor** – Placement probability and risk level  
- 🧠 **Explainable AI** – Know which factors helped or hurt your placement chances  
- 📊 **Analytics & Dashboard** – Cool charts & graphs for faculty & placement officers  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack (aka The Wizardry Behind the Curtain)

- **Frontend**: React.js, Chart.js  
- **Backend**: Spring Boot (Java)  
- **Database**: MySQL  
- **ML & AI**: Python, XGBoost / Random Forest, SHAP / LIME  
- **Excel Reports**: Apache POI  
- **Email Service**: SMTP / JavaMail
